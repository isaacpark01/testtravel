How It Works

Go from "where to?" to "let's go!" in four steps

From inspiration to itinerary — Dropped does the heavy lifting

01
Discover

Browse 50+ handpicked destinations with curated activities, restaurant picks, transport options, and local deals — filtered by price, rating, and your travel style.

02
Plan

Drag activities into a day-by-day kanban board for your solo trip, or spin up a shared Group Board and let your crew vote on ideas in real time.

03
Book

Compare flights across Google Flights, Kayak, Skyscanner, and Expedia with one click. Search rental cars and estimate your full trip budget before you commit.

04
Share & Vote

Drop the link, let the chaos begin. Share your itinerary with the whole squad, have everyone vote on what's in, and co-create the trip of a lifetime — together.
